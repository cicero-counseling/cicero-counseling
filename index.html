ABOUT US

Cicero Counseling is more than just a college guidance service—we're dedicated to empowering students to build self-aware, purposeful futures. Through personalized support and comprehensive counseling, we help you navigate the college application process, make informed career choices, and ultimately, achieve your dreams. Our mission goes beyond acceptance letters. We believe education is a transformative journey, shaping you not just academically, but personally. Developing strong character and virtue is central to our approach, as we strive to cultivate responsible and engaged citizens who will contribute meaningfully to society.

Inspired by the classical liberal arts tradition, we emphasize a well-rounded education that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and empathy. This empowers you to explore diverse perspectives, articulate your voice, and ultimately, forge your unique path. Join Cicero, and embark on a journey of self-discovery and academic excellence. Together, let's build a future that reflects your passions and empowers your voice.

Our Services

Profile Building

Craft a college application that showcases your unique strengths and passions. Our profile building experts help you explore interests, identify leadership opportunities, and tailor activities to impress admissions committees. Gain confidence and present your best self to dream schools. Ready to shine? Schedule a consultation today!

Essay support

Our experienced essay coaches guide you through every step, from brainstorming captivating topics to crafting polished prose. Overcome writer's block, refine your voice, and express your genuine self—all while meeting deadlines with confidence. Write an essay that stands out, get accepted to your dream schools, and start your incredible journey.

College Selection

Our college selection experts help you discover institutions that align with your academic desires, personal goals, and financial needs. We analyze your profile, explore diverse options, and guide you through the application process with strategic decision-making. Find your dream school and embark on aN academic adventure. Schedule a consultation today!

Appliction Assistance

Feeling overwhelmed by application forms, deadlines, and requirements? Breathe easy! Our application assistance experts guide you through the entire process, from gathering documents to proofreading essays. We ensure accuracy, completeness, and timely submission, so you can focus on excelling in your academics. Achieve application perfection and get ready for exciting possibilities. Get in touch today!

ED Guidance

Our early decision guidance experts ensure you make a strategic, informed choice to maximize your chances of acceptance. We analyze your profile, assess competitiveness, and guide you through the entire process - from crafting compelling applications to navigating financial aid. Unlock early acceptance and kickstart your dream academic journey. Schedule a consultation today!

Deadline Management

Juggling applications, tests, and schoolwork? Don't let deadlines stress you out! Our deadline management experts equip you with tools and strategies to stay on top of everything. We help you create personalized calendar systems, prioritize tasks, and develop proactive habits to avoid last-minute scrambles. Free yourself from deadline anxiety, schedule a consultation today!
